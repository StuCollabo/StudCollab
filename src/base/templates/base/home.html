StuCollab's story

Mathieu MATONGO is a young computer science student. When reviewing his group’s work, he noticed that people tend to form groups with others who live close to each other.

Mathieu therefore came up with the idea of a platform where students — and potentially other types of users — can collaborate, send documents, assign tasks, or even work together in real time on the same document.
